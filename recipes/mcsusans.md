title: mcsusans
description: chocolate chip cookies
ingredients:
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 1/4 cup flour
- 2 cups chocolate chips
---

- mix all ingredients except flour and chips
- mix in flour
- mix in chocolate chips
- bake 375 F for 7-9 minutes
